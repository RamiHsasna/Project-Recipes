Pesto

Description

Pesto is an Italian cuisine that is popular in Italy. It is a popular cuisine in Italy because it is popular in Italy.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion & Oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
